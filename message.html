You were 15 when I was born and by the time I could form memories (around 3 years old) you were already a grown man. Having to grow up without a dad who was physically with us wasn’t always easy and was never ideal. But consciously, and oftentimes unconsciously, I looked to you as the leader to show the rest of us the way through the void that was left behind. You did an excellent job of setting the standard and your impact on who I grew up to be can’t be measured.
